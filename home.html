Recreation

We have loads of activites avaliable

Hiking

There are many trails around town. Whether it be hiking, cycling, going on an ATV adventure or going on a walk with your dogs there is a trail for you. There are many diffrent trails ones that take you to mountain peaks, trails by the beach or by the river. There are also many camping spots if you want to go on a off-the-grid adventure.

Watercraft Rentals

There are many Marinas to rent watercraft's from. There are boats and yatch rentals if you want to go boating with family and friends. There are also jetski, paddle board rentals for other water activities. If you have your own watercrafts there are many boat launch sites around town.

Fishing

The town also has many fishing activites to choose from. There are many wonderful peirs and river fishing spots for those who want to stay on land. You can also rent boats and go to the many lakes or rivers to fish. There are also many ice-fishing spots to check out in the winter.

Skiing

The town also has many activites for the winter. There are also many ski resorts in the area. There are many skiing or snowboarding trails for any skill levels. There are also tubing hills you can go to with the whole family.
